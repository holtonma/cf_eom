EyeOn-Future-Major Contestant Update :: Todd Demsey at Nationwide Tour's Cox Classic : 67-69-68 (-9)


PGA Tour pro and Nationwide Tour Pro, Todd Demsey....past NCAA Champion, undefeated Walker Cup Member, and college teammate of Phil Mickelson at ASU.... took some time out of his busy schedule today to answer some questions from the EyeOnMajors.com Committee via telephone interview about what it will take to win this year's PGA Championship, which players he likes to contend that week at Baltusrol, what characteristics to look for in a player to contend at this year's major, who he likes to contend coming off recent Nationwide Tour successes, why Phil is always a threat, and more... He also gives us what his 6 picks are in our game, given the EyeOnMajors.com handicaps. We hope you enjoy this unique insight when making your selections on eyeonmajors.com!
